Every platform, every application

Backup at file, application or machine level

MAX Backup seamlessly integrates with today’s most popular operating systems and applications to provide you the flexibility to backup as little as one file, a specific application (such as Exchange), an entire Windows server, VMware or Hyper-V images and data, or anything in between.

Recover Anything Faster

Backup and operational, no matter the need

Flexible recovery options

Choice is a good thing

You want the ability to recover whatever they need to whenever they need it. MAX Backup supports file level recovery as well as volumes, applications, or machines with physical, physical-to-virtual (P2V), and virtual-to-virtual (V2V) recovery all supported.

Cloud recovery target

Bring your own cloud

Not every customer, or MSP, has the physical or virtual infrastructure to recover to when disaster strikes. But some critical systems require an ability to recover to a virtual server quickly in the event of a failure or disaster. Being able to use public cloud providers gives your customers an additional recovery option neither of you had, increasing your speed of recovery and reestablishing business operations.

Continuous recovery

Virtual recovery before you need it

You don’t need to wait on for a disaster in order to start a recovery. MAX Backup can automatically send daily delta changes to a VM, keeping it continuously updated. So when a failure happens, recovery is just a click away. Business continuity as it should be.

Secure multi-tenant architecture

Many backups. Many clients. One solution.

You don’t have time to manage each client’s backup requirements separately. Backup and recovery activities for all your clients are managed from a single pane of glass while ensuring that the integrity and security of individual customer data isn’t compromised.

Security-focused storage

Ultra-safe. ultra-secure.

Your customers’ data is stored in our certified data centers worldwide. You choose in which country to store your customers' data and that’s where it stays, regardless of who comes asking for a copy. Private key encryption

Client-side only data access

Once backed up, only your customers should be able to access their data. Private key encryption (AES 128-bit, AES 256-bit, or 448-bit Blowfish) ensures only the owner of the key – your customer - can recover and access their data, eliminating any unauthorized access.
